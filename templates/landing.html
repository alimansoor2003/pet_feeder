The Product

One feeder. Every pet, recognized.

A single camera-equipped feeder watches the bowl, detects when an animal approaches, and matches their face against your registered pets — then decides whether to dispense food based on your rules.

  • Works with any number of dogs or cats in the home
  • Runs entirely offline — no cloud dependency required
  • Feeding limits enforced automatically (portions, schedule, daily caps)
  • Full history available in your personal dashboard

How It Works

From photo to feeding decision in four simple steps.

1

Register Your Pet

Upload one clear photo of each pet to your dashboard.

2

Detect the Animal

YOLO object detection checks whether a dog or cat is at the bowl.

3

Identify the Pet

An embedding model matches them against your registered pets.

4

Feed, Within Limits

Feeding is allowed or paused based on schedule and daily limits.
