📝 Register a Pet

Add a new pet to the feeder database. We'll use the photo to identify your pet during feeding events.

📸 Best results: clear face shot, good lighting, minimal background

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Clear photo: Make sure your pet's face is visible and in focus
  • Good lighting: Natural light works best
  • Close-up: Crop close enough to see facial features
  • Neutral background: Simpler backgrounds help identification
  • Still pose: Motion blur reduces accuracy
  • Multiple angles: Register from a typical viewing angle
